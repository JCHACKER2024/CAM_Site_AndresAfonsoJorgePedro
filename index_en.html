The Image of the Future!

The Bachelor's Degree in Audiovisual Communication and Multimedia (CAM), launched in the 2009/2010 academic year, offers a highly dynamic education. Its academic path combines a stimulating theoretical and practical learning experience centered on Cinema and Video, along with interdisciplinary training in audiovisual and multimedia creation and technologies associated with new media. Constantly updated, we foster both individual and teamwork through practical projects in photography, filmmaking, web creation, sound design, screenwriting, acting direction, multimedia installation, and video mapping. The department organizes the annual Multiplex event, which features a lecture by an international guest and the premiere of final-year student films, in partnership with the Batalha Centro de Cinema.
