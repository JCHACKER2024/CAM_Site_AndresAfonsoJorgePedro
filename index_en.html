The Image Of the Future!

The Bachelor's Degree in Audiovisual Communication and Multimedia has shown great dynamism, offering an academic path that combines a stimulating theoretical-practical learning component based on Cinema and Video, with interdisciplinary training in audiovisual and multimedia creation and technologies associated with new media. Constantly updated, as required by the field, it explores individual and team work in practical projects in the areas of photography, video and short film, web creation, sound design, screenwriting and acting direction, multimedia installations, and video mapping. The course is responsible for the MULTIPLEX event, an Annual Moving Image Cycle, held in partnership with the BATALHA Film Center.
