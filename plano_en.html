STUDY PLAN

The Bachelor's degree in Audiovisual Communication and Multimedia focuses on comprehensive training, providing students with the ability to respond to multiple areas of creative production. Learn more about the three-act journey of your degree's film.

1st ACT: CONFIGURATION

The Beginning - Learn the theory and practical foundations.

1st Semester - Act I.I
Photography Theory

Explore the history and theoretical basis of photography.

Photographic Practices

Work with photography in a practical way, as an author.

Digital Image

Learn basic tools for image creation.

History of Media

Explore the evolution of Communication Media.

Web Applications

How to create and communicate on the web?

2nd Semester - Act I.II
Introduction to Design

Understand notions of design, color, balance, and legibility.

Interaction Design

Learn the concepts of interaction and usability.

Introduction to Multimedia Technologies

Discover the potential of multimedia applications.

Cinema Theory

Cinema analysis and theory at the BATALHA Film Center cinema hall.

Video and Audio Operations

Learn to professionally capture sound and image.

2nd ACT: CONFRONTATION

The Middle - Explore different branches of Audiovisual Communication and Multimedia.

1st Semester - Act II.I
Visual Culture

Develop the way you observe and analyze images.

Cinematography I

Learn to control light and shoot the most cinematic shots.

Editing and Post-Production

Adapt to different editing styles and elevate the look of your videos.

Screenwriting

Practice narrative foundations for writing scripts.

Sound Design

Learn to create immersive sound environments.

2nd Semester - Act II.II
Multimedia Product Design

Design effective multimedia experiences.

Acting Direction

In partnership with the Bachelor's in Dramatic Arts.

Cinematography II

Deeply explore cinematography in short films.

Media Culture Economics

Management and economics applied to new media.

Multimedia Technologies

VR, AR, 360 video, and 3D Scanning.

3rd ACT: RESOLUTION

The End - It's time to witness the film's climax.

1st Semester - Act III.I
2D Animation

Explore the potential of digital animation and motion graphics.

3D Modeling and Animation

Create three-dimensional objects and worlds.

Contemporary Arts

Get to know the current panorama of Contemporary Art.

Advanced Scriptwriting

Prepare the script for your final course projects.

2nd Semester - Act III.II
Audiovisual Creation Project

Finish the course with your most impactful films.

Multimedia Creation Project

Create your project to be exhibited in a Contemporary Art gallery.

Project Seminar and Professional Integration

Prepare for professional life with a project or internship.
