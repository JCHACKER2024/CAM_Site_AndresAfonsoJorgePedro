MULTIPLEX

Multiplex - Moving Image Cycle is an annual event of great importance for the CAM course, counting on the collaboration of students and teachers for its realization. It has taken several formats, including Workshops, Masterclasses, Thematic Cycles, and Exhibitions, with various partner institutions such as Cinema Passos Manuel, Maus Hábitos, Teatro Nacional do Porto Rivoli, Espaço Mira, and currently, Batalha Centro de Cinema. We are proud to have brought to the city of Porto a group of filmmakers and artists of the highest international significance to share their knowledge and cinematography.
