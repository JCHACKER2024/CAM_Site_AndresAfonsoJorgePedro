Bachelor in Audiovisual and Multimedia Communication

Film and Media Arts | Porto

Course format: In-Person Degree: Bachelor Semesters: 6 Credits: 180

What will I learn in this course?

This degree has shown great academic dynamism, with immediate adhesion by a large number of enrolled students (Portuguese, but also students of Brazilian, Angolan and Cape Verdean origin). It offers an academic path that combines a stimulating theoretical-practical learning component based on Cinema and Video, with interdisciplinary training in the area of audiovisual and multimedia creation and technologies associated with new media. In constant updating, as required by the area, individual and team work is explored in practical projects in the areas of photography, video and short film, web creation, sound design, scriptwriting and directing actors, multimedia installations and video mapping. The course is responsible for Multiplex, Annual Cycle of Moving Images

Technical and professional skills that I will acquire

The Multimedia marks the human habitat, without audiovisual and multimedia the world would be unrecognizable, and in our existential reality, uninhabitable. Thus, the degree of Audiovisual Communication and Multimedia bet on a comprehensive training, equipping the student responsiveness to multiple areas in which communication and audiovisual media currently operate. These areas include the development, writing and producing projects filmic, television and videographic fiction; documentary, institutional and advertising, production, production and executive production assistance projects filmic, television and videographic fiction, documentary, institutional and advertising; film directing, lighting and imaging assistance; operations in camera film technology, film and video, image control and operations vt; audio post-production, sound capture and sound design, post production and visual composition in filmic projects, television and videographic drama, documentary, institutional and advertising; analogue and digital photography, promotion and distribution of audiovisual and multimedia project management and conceção multimedia, development and writing projects, consultancy projects in multimedia and information systems; design applications, interfaces and information for multimedia platforms (web, offline, mobile) and realization of projects of animation, modeling and 2D and 3D animation, multimedia programming environments, systems analysis and design multimedia, graphic design, typesetting and production of infographics.

Infrastructure and Equipment

Universidade Lusófona do Porto has the necessary equipment for the convenient teaching of the syllabus of the Study Cycle, as well as a studio, photography, video editing, audio recording and editing labs, as well as an audiovisual warehouse. All spaces and equipment can be requested and used by students from the first year of the degree.
