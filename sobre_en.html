Live Creativity

CAM is a Bachelor's degree that offers a wide range of experiences reaching far beyond the university walls, being a course open to the city and the world. We aim to broaden horizons and stand at the forefront of the image of the future. Discover the spaces and events that define our course.

EVENTS

Multiplex

Multiplex is an event created by the CAM course, with its first edition in 2011. It hosts film cycles and masterclasses featuring major international filmmakers and artists such as Nan Goldin, Agnès Varda, Victor Erice, ORLAN, Ildikó Enyedi, Sara Driver, and Manoel de Oliveira, among many others. Explore all editions here.

PARTNERSHIP

Batalha Film Center

We have the immense privilege of being located right next to one of the city's most iconic cinemas, the Batalha Centro de Cinema. This provides our students with the excellent opportunity to view, analyze, study, and attend film classes inside a real movie theater throughout the year! In partnership with Batalha, we organize the monthly GREAT ARTISTS ON CAMPUS cycle during the academic year, welcoming prestigious guest filmmakers and multimedia artists such as Vhils, Mikhail Karikis, Mila Turajlić, and Diana Policarpo, among many others.

GALLERY

Espaço Mira

Our students benefit from another unique opportunity by attending and showcasing their multimedia installations at Espaço Mira, one of the most iconic contemporary art galleries in the city. They do so not merely as students, but as active artists within the city's cultural landscape.
