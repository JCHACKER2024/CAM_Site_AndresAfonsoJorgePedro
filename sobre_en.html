LIVING IN CREATIVITY

CAM is a Bachelor's degree that allows you to have a multiplicity of experiences that go beyond the university walls, being a course open to the city and the world. We want to broaden horizons and be at the forefront of the image of the future. Discover the spaces and events that define our course.

Annual Event

MULTIPLEX

Multiplex is an event created by the CAM course that offers students and the city of Porto the opportunity to enjoy thematic Film Cycles and Masterclasses with major world filmmakers and artists such as Ildikó Enyedi, Nan Goldin, Sara Driver, ORLAN, and Manoel de Oliveira. Discover all Multiplex editions here.

Partnership

BATALHA FILM CENTER

We have the great privilege of being located next to one of the city's most iconic film centers, Batalha, offering the excellent opportunity to view, analyze, study, and attend cinema classes in a real movie theater. We also feature weekly "Great Artists on Campus" Masterclasses with guest artists such as Vhils, Mikhail Karikis, Mila Turajlić, and Diana Policarpo.

Gallery

ESPAÇO MIRA

Our students enjoy another unique opportunity, witnessing and exhibiting their multimedia installations at Espaço Mira, one of the most emblematic contemporary art galleries in the city. They do this not just as students, but as artists of the city.
